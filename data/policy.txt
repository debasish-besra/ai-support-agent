Zomato Refund Policy:
Orders cancelled within 5 minutes are eligible for full refund.
Orders cancelled after 5 minutes but before dispatch are eligible for 50% refund.
Orders cancelled after dispatch are not eligible for refund.

Zomato Delivery Policy:
Standard delivery takes 30-45 minutes.
Express delivery takes 15-20 minutes and costs 30 rupees extra.
Free delivery on orders above 299 rupees.

Zomato Payment Policy:
We accept UPI, credit cards, debit cards, and Zomato credits.
Cash on delivery is available in select cities.
Zomato credits never expire.
